Our Mission
As soon-to-be graduates, we’ve learned that newly grads and those without much experience in the real-world environment have difficulty searching for jobs. Our mission is to build a bridge between the workforce and newly graduates, beginners, and newcomers.
What Our Application Does
Our application allows users to look for job postings and listings catered to them. The app will also keep track of the user’s applications and listings.
